Hybrid Shape Sorter

This idea came to us through a math video on youtube. My husband loves math videos and is constantly watching them in his free time, and it finally paid off. We watched a video where a man with a resin printer was expaining intersectional shapes and how you could potentially make a kids toy out of it. However, he couldn't find a geometry where each side could only fit into it's respective hole.

We ended up using the (insert part from rhett) function in CAD to find the overlapping cross section between a cone, cross, and pentagon.

The 3D block could be rotated to fit into each of the three holes, and our kids loved figuring out how this one block could fit into so many different shapes.
